How to log in and get started

1

Open the portal using the button below

Click "Go to Learning Portal" at the bottom of this page. The portal will open in a new tab. You can also use the direct link sent to your email.

2

Log in with your credentials

Enter the username and password from the email you received from the course provider. Your username is typically your email address.

💡 Tip — copy and paste your password from the email to avoid typos
3

Locate your course in the portal

Once logged in, navigate to My Courses or your dashboard. Your course should appear there automatically. If it doesn't, contact support.

4

Change your password on first login

For security, the portal may prompt you to set a new password on your first login. Choose a strong password and store it securely.

💡 Tip — use a password manager or note it somewhere safe for future logins
